Exercise 3.29: List some of the drawbacks of using an array instead of a vector.

Arrays are fixed in size.
Until recently, arrays needed to be initialized with a constexpr.
Arrays cannot be copied.
Character arrays need space for a null value.
You cannot use bracket notationto initiate an array (10, 42).
